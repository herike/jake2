Jake2 NApplet

  • ESC - release the game mouse grab.
  • HOME - in/out browser window.
  • Video Mode Size change will change the Applet size.
  • Fullscreen will bring the window out of the browser as well and MonitorMode is applied to the main MonitorDevice.
Bugs:
  • OSX: Fullscreen may freeze the JVM/Desktop, due to CGL.setContextView() deadlock
